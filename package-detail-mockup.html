About Krystal Cancun

Krystal Cancun is a beachfront resort located at the heart of the Hotel Zone, steps from Cancún's famous turquoise waters. The property features a stunning outdoor pool complex, multiple restaurants serving Mexican and international cuisine, and direct access to a white-sand beach.

Rooms are spacious and contemporary with floor-to-ceiling windows offering views of the Caribbean Sea or the lagoon. The hotel is well-positioned for both relaxation and exploring — Cancún's nightlife strip, La Isla Shopping Village, and the famous Mayan ruins of Chichén Itzá are all within reach.

The resort caters to couples, families, and solo travellers alike. A full-service spa, gym, multiple bars, and a dedicated kids' area make it versatile for any type of trip. Concierge services can arrange tours, water sports, and airport transfers.

The Hotel Zone location puts guests within walking distance of dozens of restaurants, nightclubs, and shops, while the beach and pool provide a serene retreat from the buzz of the city.
